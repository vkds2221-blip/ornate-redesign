Our Story

Technology partner
with a purpose

Ornate TechnoServices began as an offshore outsourcing proprietorship in 2009 and became a Private Limited company in 2011. Over 16 years, we've grown into one of India's trusted multi-certified IT partners — serving PSUs, enterprises, and startups nationwide.

We are accredited by Microsoft, ISO, and hold CMMI Level 3 — a standard achieved by fewer than 5% of IT companies globally. Our work spans government, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, and education.
